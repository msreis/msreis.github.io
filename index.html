Welcome!
The Dynamic Systems Biology Group, leaded by Marcelo S. Reis and hosted at Butantan Institute, has as a main goal the development of cutting-edge Bayesian Model Selection and Machine Learning techniques and their application into the modeling of biological systems, with emphasis to their underlying dynamic mechanisms. Currently, we are focused on three types of problems: the modeling of cell signaling pathways in the context of cancer biology; the deciphering of the DNA replication dynamics in trypanosomatids; the inference of phyloproteomic cladograms from mass spectrometry (MS)-based proteomic assays.
